## jq Queries:

query-1-1.jq:
Finds departments with teachers who teach multiple courses and calculates their average teaching ratings.

query-1-2.jq:
For each department, compares course popularity with department average and classifies each course as above, below, or equal to average.

query-2-1.jq:
Groups students by program, showing enrollment years and student names for each program.

query-2-2.jq:
Shows two analyses:
1. Individual student enrollment patterns sorted by year and semester
2. Statistics about semesters including count of enrollments and years when offered
